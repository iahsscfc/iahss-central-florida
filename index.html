About our chapter

Strength through a diverse professional community

The IAHSS Central Florida Chapter brings together professionals from small hospitals, major healthcare systems, in-house security programs, contract security companies and the organizations that support them.

That diversity creates a broad network of experience and expertise members can rely on.
